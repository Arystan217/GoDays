    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

    Welcome to our FAQs section, where we address the most common queries and concerns about GoDays. We believe in transparency and want to ensure that you have all the information you need to make an informed decision about our platform.

    What is GoDays?
    GoDays is an AI-powered platform designed to prevent employee burnout and identify hidden leaders within organizations. Our innovative algorithms analyze keyboard and mouse usage, proactively detecting signs of burnout and distributing tasks intelligently to optimize workloads.
    How does GoDays prevent burnout?
    GoDays adapts workload based on employees' keyboard and mouse usage, ensuring a healthy work-life balance. By reducing the risk of burnout and providing timely support, GoDays promotes employee well-being and enhances productivity.
    Can GoDays integrate with existing CRM and task management systems?
    Absolutely! GoDays seamlessly integrates with your current CRM and task management tools, ensuring a smooth transition without disrupting established workflows. Enhance productivity and collaboration with our user-friendly platform that fits right into your organizational processes.

    Is my data safe with GoDays?
